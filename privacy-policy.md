# Privacy Policy for AA Split

Last updated: February 2026

## Overview
AA Split ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Data Collection
AA Split does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter in the app (bill names, member names, expenses) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Storage
- All app data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- We do not have access to your data
- Your data is never sent to any server or third party

## Sharing Features
When you use the "Generate Settlement Card" feature, the image is generated locally on your device. Sharing is done through your device's native share function. We do not receive or store any shared content.

## Third-Party Services
AA Split does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

## Children's Privacy
AA Split does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
